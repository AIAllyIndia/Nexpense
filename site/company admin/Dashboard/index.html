    Dashboard

    The Company Admin Dashboard gives admins a quick summary of everything happening in their company account.
    It shows key numbers, shortcuts, and important updates in one place.

    When you log in, you will see a welcome message with your name.

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_overview_v1.PNG]]

    Dashboard Summary Cards

    1. Total Users: Shows the total number of users created in your company. 2. Employees: Displays how many employees are currently added to the system. 3. Total Reports: Shows the number of spend/claim reports submitted by employees. 4. Pending Approvals: Indicates how many reports are waiting for admin approval. 5. Total Spend This Month: Shows the total company spend recorded for the current month.

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_summary_cards_v1.png]]

    Top Menu Shortcuts

    • Analytics

    The Analytics section gives company admins a clear view of how the system is being used.
    It shows important usage metrics, trends, and activity summaries in one place.

    You can access it from the main dashboard by clicking the Analytics button.

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_analytics_overview_v1.png]]

    What You Can See in Analytics

    1. Number of Users: Shows how many users are currently registered under your company.
    2. Costs/Spends Created: Displays the total number of spending entries added by employees.
    3. **Submitted Reports: ** Shows how many spend reports have been submitted for approval.
    4. Approved Reports: Displays the count of reports that have been reviewed and approved.
    5. Storage Used: Shows how much storage has been used for uploaded receipts and documents.
    6. Monthly Spend: Displays the total amount your company has spent in the current month.

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_analytics_1_v1.png]] 1. Reports by Status: This chart shows how many reports fall under each category: - Approved – Reports that have been reviewed and approved - In Approval – Reports currently being checked - Rejected – Reports that were not approved - Submitted – Reports that employees have submitted This helps admins quickly see the overall status of all reports.

    2. Monthly Spend Trend: This line graph shows how much your company has spent every month. It helps you understand: - Spending patterns - Months with higher or lower spending
    - Overall budget trends The graph updates automatically based on submitted reports.

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_analytics_2_v1.png]]

    3. Storage Growth (GB)

    This chart shows how much storage has been used month-by-month.

    Storage is used for things like:

    • Uploaded receipts

    • Bills

    • Documents

    You can also select the year from the dropdown to view data for different years.

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_analytics_3_v1.png]]

    • Logs

    The Activity Logs page shows every action taken by users inside the company account.
    It helps admins track changes, monitor activity, and maintain transparency.

    You can use this page to quickly see who did what and when.

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_logs_1_v1.PNG]]

    Search & Filters

    At the top, you can filter logs using multiple options: • Search Bar: Type any keyword to find specific logs. • Action Types: Filter by actions such as: - Spend Updated - Spend Created - Report Created - User Updated - Login Activity
    and more.

    • Entity Types: Filter based on what was changed: - Spend - Report
    - User
    - Department
    - Company
    etc. • Users: Select a specific user to see only their activity. • Start Date / End Date: Choose a date range to view logs for a certain period. • Refresh Button: Click to reload the latest activity.

    Logs Table Overview

    Each log entry shows: 1. Timestamp: The exact date and time when the action happened. 2. User: The user who performed the action. 3. Event Type: The type of activity performed (e.g., Spend Updated, Report Created, User Updated). 4. Description: A short explanation of what happened. 5. Actions: Click the eye icon to view more details about that log entry.

    Why Activity Logs are Useful

    • Track changes made by employees
    • Monitor suspicious or unexpected actions
    • Verify updates to spends, reports, and user accounts
    • Maintain a transparent audit trail for compliance

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_logs_2_v1.png]]

    • Settings

    Opens company settings like users, departments, policies, and templates.

    Click here to know more about [[docs/company admin/Settings|Settings]]

    Refresh Button

    Click the refresh icon to update dashboard numbers instantly.

    Latest Reports & Recent Activity

    1. Latest Reports: This panel shows the most recently created or updated spend reports from employees. For each report, you can see: • Report Name:Example: jan expense, example 2, AI Scanned Receipt • Employee Name: Shows who submitted the report. Example: Piyush Chauhan, Raja Babu • Amount: The total spend amount for that report. • Status: Examples: - Pending_approval_l1 - Pending_approval_l2 - Changes_requested These help you understand where each report currently stands.

    • View Button (Eye Icon): Click this to open and review the report immediately. - View All → Opens the full list of reports in detail.

    2. Recent Activity: This panel shows the most recent actions performed in the company account. For each activity, you can see: • User Name: Example: Piyush Chauhan, Raja Babu • Activity Description: Examples: - Report Pending_approval_l2 - Report Changes_requested - Report Pending_approval_l1 • Timestamp: Shows the exact date and time the action happened. • Report Button: Quick shortcut to open the related report. - View All → Opens the complete activity timeline for detailed viewing.

    Why This Section Is Helpful

    • Quickly review the most recent spend reports
    • Track pending approvals
    • Monitor employee activity in real-time
    • Jump directly to any report with one click

    ![[nexpense_company_dashboard_overview_2_v1.png]]

    Quick Actions

    1. Manage Users Quickly add, edit, or remove users in your company.
    Use this to manage employee accounts, roles, and access levels.

    2. Departments

    Create and organize departments inside your company.
    Useful for grouping employees and managing department-wise workflows.

    3. View Reports

    Open the full list of submitted spending reports.
    You can review details, check statuses, and approve reports from here.

    1. [[docs/company admin/Settings|Settings]]

    Access company-level configurations such as theme, language, currency, and other preferences.
