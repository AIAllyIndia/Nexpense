    Dashboard

    When an employee logs in to NexPense, the Dashboard opens by default. It gives you a quick summary of your expenses, reports, and approvals.

    ![[nexpense_employee_click_dashboard.png|250]]

    • From the left-side menu, click Dashboard.

    • The Dashboard screen will open.

    ![[nexpense_employee_dashboard_overview.png]]

    Summary Cards (Top Boxes)

    These boxes show important numbers at a glance:

    Total Spent

    • Shows the total amount of expenses you have added

    Pending Approvals

    • Shows the number of reports waiting for approval

    Rejected Reports

    • Shows how many reports were rejected `

    Reports Submitted

    • Shows the total number of reports you have submitted

    **Expense Breakdown

    The Expense Breakdown section shows a visual summary of your expenses.
    It helps you understand where your money is spent.

    How to Use This Section

    1. Open the Dashboard

    2. Go to Expense Breakdown

    3. Select a view from the dropdown:

      • Category / Report / Cost Centre / Project

    ![[nexpense_employee_dashboard_filter.png]]

    1. Analyze your spending visually ![[nexpense_employee_expensebreakdown.png]]

    Recent Reports

    The Recent Reports section shows a list of your latest expense reports.
    It helps you quickly check the status and amount of each report.

    • Scroll to see Recent Reports

    • Click any report to:

      • View details

      • Edit (if Draft)

      • Track approval status

    ![[nexpense_employee_recent_expenses.png]]
