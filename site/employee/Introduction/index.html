    Introduction

    NexPense is a smart and easy-to-use expense management system designed to help employees and companies manage expenses smoothly and accurately.

    With NexPense, you can:

    • Record and track your daily expenses

    • Upload receipts easily

    • Create and submit expense reports

    • View approval status in real time

    • Get clear insights into your spending

    The platform reduces manual work, avoids errors, and saves time by automating the entire expense process.

    Login to NexPense

    ![[nexpense_employee_log_in.png]]

    To access NexPense:

    1. Enter your registered email address

    2. Enter your password

    3. (Optional) Select Remember this device for quicker login next time

    4. Click Sign In

    Forgot Password – How to Reset Your Password

    If you forget your NexPense password, you can reset it easily by following these steps:

    Steps to Reset Password

    1. On the Login page, click “Forgot password?”

    2. The Forgot Password screen will open

    3. Enter your registered email address

    4. Click “Send Reset Link”

    ![[nexpense_employee-forgot_pass.png|300]]

    • A password reset link will be sent to your email

    • Open your email and click the reset link

    • Create a new password

    - Return to the login page and sign in with your new password

    • Go Back to Login

    • Click “Back to Login” if you remember your password and want to sign in normally

    Sign In

    • Tap the Sign In button after entering your email and password

    • You will be taken to the Dashboard after successful login
