    Introduction

    Welcome to NexPense - Your Smart Expense Management Solution! This user manual will guide you through all the features and functionalities of the NexPense mobile app, from initial login to managing your expenses effectively.

    Choose Your App Theme & Color

    1. Premium App Theme

    a. Pick a color that you like.
    b. This color will be used across the app buttons, headers, and highlights.

    ![[nexpense_select_theme1.jpeg|300]]

    2. Web Consistent Theme

    • Looks the same as the Nexpense web app

    • Easy to use if you already use Nexpense on the web

    ![[Nexpense_select_webtheme.jpeg|350]]

    Select Your Language

    • Tap on your preferred language

    • A ✔ check mark will appear on the selected language

    • Tap Continue to proceed

    ![[Nexpense_select_Lan1.jpeg|300]]

    Welcome Back – Sign In to Nexpense

    This screen allows you to log in to your Nexpense account and continue managing your daily expenses.

    ![[nexpense_signin.png|300]]

    Login Fields

    Email

    • Enter the email address you used while registering

    • Make sure the email is typed correctly

    Password

    • Enter your account password

    • Tap the eye icon to show or hide your password

    Additional Options

    Remember Me

    • Turn this on to stay logged in

    • Saves time when opening the app next time

    Forgot Password?

    • Tap this if you don’t remember your password

    • Follow the steps to reset your password securely

    ![[nexpense_forgot_pass.jpg|350]]

    If you have forgotten your password, don’t worry.
    You can reset it easily using your registered email address.

    Enter Your Email

    • Type the email address you used to create your Nexpense account

    • Make sure the email is correct

    Send Reset Link

    1. After entering your email, tap Send Reset Link

    2. A password reset link will be sent to your email

    3. Open the email and follow the instructions to create a new password

    ![[nexpense_forgot_pass_2.jpg|350]]

    Reset Password

    1. Enter the reset token

    2. Enter your new password

    3. Confirm the new password

    4. Tap Reset Password

    Your password will be updated successfully.

    Sign In

    • Tap the Sign In button after entering your email and password

    • You will be taken to the Dashboard after successful login
