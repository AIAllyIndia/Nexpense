### **1. Contact Us**

#### **Email Support**

If you need help, you can contact our support team via email.

- **Response Time:** Within 24 hours
    
- **Email:** operations@aially.in
    

Clicking the email option opens your default mail app to compose a support request.

#### **Support Hours**

You can reach out to the support team during the following hours:

- **Monday – Friday:** 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM IST
    
- **Saturday – Sunday:** Closed
    
- **Emergency Support:** Available 24/7 for critical issues
