### **Dark Mode**

Use this option to switch between **Light Mode** and **Dark Mode**.

- **Light Mode** – Bright background for daytime use.
    
- **Dark Mode** – Dark background for better visibility at night and reduced eye strain.
    

Just toggle the switch to change the theme instantly.

### Display Preferences

These settings control how language and currency information appear across your dashboard.
#### **Language**

Select the app language you prefer (e.g., English, Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, etc.). This helps you use the app in the language you are most comfortable with.

#### **Currency**

Choose the currency you want to use for all expenses (e.g., INR, USD, EUR, GBP, JPY). The selected currency will be used across the app for all financial values.
