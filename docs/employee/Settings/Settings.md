The **Settings** section allows employees to manage their personal preferences and customize how NexPense works for them.

---
Go to the left menu → click **Settings** (highlighted in green).  
This will open all your personal preferences in one place.

![[nexpense_employee_settings_v1.png|205]]
### **What you can do:**

- **View & update your profile**  
    Change allowed details like name or contact info.
    
- **Change language**  
    Select your preferred language for the app.
    
- **Change display currency**  
    Choose how amounts are shown in your dashboard and reports.
    
- **Manage notifications**  
    Turn email or web-push notifications on or off.
    
- **Update password**  
    Change your login password (if allowed by your company).
    
![[nexpense_employee_settings_overview_v1.png]]

---
### **Profile**

The **Profile** section lets you view and update your personal information.
##### **What you can do here:**

- **View your basic details**  
    See your name, email, employee code, company, and department.
    
- **Update your personal info**  
    Edit fields like your full name or phone number (if allowed by your company).
    
- **Keep your account accurate**  
    Make sure your contact details are up to date for notifications and verification.
##### **How to update:**

1. Edit the fields you want to change.
    
2. Click **Update Profile** to save.

![[nexpense_employee_settings_profile_v1.png]]

---
### Notifications
Manage how you receive updates in the app.

##### **What you can control:**

- **Email Notifications** – Get updates by email.
    
- **Push Notifications** – Receive alerts on your browser or mobile.
    
- **Expense Updates** – Get notified when your expenses are processed.
    
- **Report Status** – Alerts for report approvals or rejections.
    
- **Approval Alerts** – Notifications when something needs your approval.
    

Toggle each option **ON/OFF** based on your preference.

![[nexpense_employee_settings_notifications_v1.png]]

### **Preferences**

Customize how the app looks and works for you.

##### **What you can change:**

- **Dark Mode** – Switch between light and dark themes.
    
- **Language** – Choose the language you prefer for the app.
    
- **Currency** – Set the default currency for viewing expenses.
    
- **Date Format** – Select how dates should appear (e.g., DD/MM/YYYY).
    
- **Auto-Save** – Automatically save your drafts while working.
    

Use these options to personalize your experience and make the app easier to use.

### **Security**

Use this section to update your account password and keep your profile safe.

##### **Change Password**

- Enter your **current password**
    
- Set a **new password**
    
- Confirm the new password
    
- Click **Change Password** to save
    

**Security Tips**

- Use at least 8 characters
    
- Include numbers, symbols, and mixed case letters
    
- Never share your password with anyone

### **FAQ**

The FAQ section provides quick answers to common questions about using NexPense.

##### **What You’ll Find**

- **How do I submit expenses?**  
    Create a report, attach your expenses with receipts, and submit it for approval.
    
- **How long does approval take?**  
    Usually 2–3 business days, depending on your company’s policy.
    
- **Can I edit after submission?**  
    You can edit only if your manager returns the report for changes. Draft expenses are always editable.
