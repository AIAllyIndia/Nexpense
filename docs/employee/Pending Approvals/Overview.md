The **Pending Approvals** section allows managers or approvers to review all expenses that require their action.

##### **What You Can Do Here**

- **View all expenses awaiting approval**  
    See employee submissions that need your review.
    
- **Open any expense for detailed review**  
    Check receipts, amounts, categories, and other details.
    
- **Approve, Reject, or Request Changes**
    
    - **Approve** → Accept the expense and move it forward
        
    - **Reject** → Decline the submission with a reason
        
    - **Request Changes** → Ask the employee to correct or update information
        

##### **Where to Find It**

From the left menu, click **Pending Approvals** to open your approval dashboard.

![[nexpense_employee_pending_approvals_v1.png|250]]

#### **1. Summary Cards (Top Section)**

These cards show a quick count of all approval categories:

- **Pending Approvals** – Items waiting for your review
    
- **Approved by Me** – Items you have approved
    
- **Rejected by Me** – Items you declined
    
- **Changes Requested** – Items you sent back for updates
    

This gives you an instant snapshot of your workflow.

---

### **2. Detailed Approval Sections**

Each category below provides more detail and access to the respective lists:

#### **Pending Approvals**

- View all reports needing immediate action
    
- Click **View Details** to review, approve, reject, or request changes
    

#### **Approved by Me**

- Shows items you have already approved
    
- Useful for tracking past decisions
    

#### **Rejected by Me**

- Lists all reports you rejected
    
- Helps maintain approval clarity and audit compliance
    

#### **Changes Requested**

- Shows reports where you asked employees to update information
    
- You can review them again once resubmitted
    

---

### **3. Total Approvals Managed**

At the bottom, you can see:

- **Total number of approvals you’ve handled**
    
- Across all categories (approved, rejected, or changes requested)
    

This helps track your workload and approval activity.
