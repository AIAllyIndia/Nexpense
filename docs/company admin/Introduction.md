This is the login page for **Company Admins**.  
Admins use this page to securely access the Nexpense Admin Dashboard, where they can manage their company’s transaction, employees, and settings.

---
### **Login Information**

#### **Email**

Enter the email address used for your admin account.

#### **Password**

Enter your account password.  
You can tap the eye icon to show or hide the password.

#### **Remember this device**

Turn this on if you want to stay logged in on your computer.

#### **Forgot Password?**

Click this if you need to reset your password.

---

### **Sign In**

Click **Sign In** to open your Admin Dashboard.  
If you don’t have login access, contact your company administrator.

---

### **What Admins Can Do After Login**

Once you sign in, you can:

- Add and manage **employees** and **departments**
    
- Approve or reject **expense claims**
    
- Set up **expense policies** and approval rules
    
- View **real-time reports** and analytics
    
- Manage company details and subscription plans
    
- Use AI-powered tools to scan and track receipts
