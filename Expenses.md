The **Expense Details** page shows complete information about a selected expense, including amount, category, date, vendor, notes, and the attached receipt.

**How to access:**  
**Expenses → Tap any expense**

---

## **What You Can View**

### **1. Expense Summary**

- **Amount** (₹715.00)
    
- **Vendor name**
    
- Badge showing **AI Scanned Receipt**
    
- Status (e.g., Pending)
    

---

## **2. Expense Fields**

**• Category**

Shows the assigned expense category (e.g., Food).

**• Date**

Displays the date of the transaction.

**• Vendor**

Auto-filled vendor name or manually edited.

**• Notes**

Additional information added by the user.

---

## **3. Receipt / Document**

- Shows the scanned receipt or uploaded image.
    
- Tap to view or zoom in.
    

---

## **4. Actions**

### **Edit Expense**

Allows you to update:

- Amount
    
- Category
    
- Date
    
- Vendor
    
- Notes
    
- Receipt
    

### **Delete Expense**

Removes the expense permanently.
