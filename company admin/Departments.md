## How to Create a New Department

### Step 1: Click Add Department

- On the top-right corner, click **+ Add Department**

![[nexpense_company_admin_expenses_Departments__C1.1_v1.png]]

### Step 2: Create New Department Form Opens

- A popup titled **Create New Department** appears.

![[nexpense_company_admin_expenses_Departments__C1_v1.png|400]]

### Step 3: Enter Information 

- **Enter Department Name:** Type the name of the department (this field is required).
    
- **Add Department Code:** Enter a short department code if needed (optional).
    
- **Add Description:** Write a brief description about the department (optional).
    
- **Select Department Head:** Choose a user to manage the department (optional).
    
- **Choose Status:** Select whether the department should be Active or Inactive.

### Step 4: Create Department

- Click **Create Department** to save.
    
- Click **Cancel** to discard.

### Step 5: Department Created Successfully

- The new department appears as a card in **Departments Management**.
    
- You can now edit, activate/deactivate, or assign members.
