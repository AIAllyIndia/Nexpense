Notifications help admins stay informed about **important system updates and activities** in NexPense.

## **How to Open Notifications**

### **Method 1: From the Side Menu**

1. Open the **NexPense Admin Portal**.
    
2. From the **left-side menu**, click **Notifications**.
    
3. The Notifications screen will open.

![[nexpense_company_admin_leftmenunotification.png|250]]
    

---

### **Method 2: From the Bell Icon**

1. Click the **notification bell (üîî)** at the top-right corner.
    
2. You will be redirected to the Notifications page.

![[nexpense_company_admin_notification_bell.png]]

---

## **Filter Notifications**

- Use the **All Notifications** dropdown on the top-right
    
- Helps filter and view specific notification types (if available)
    

---

## **View Notification Details**

1. Click **View details** on a notification.
    
2. Full information related to that notification will be shown.
    
3. The notification is automatically marked as **read**.
    

---

## **Notification Status**

- Unread notifications appear highlighted
    
- Once opened, they are marked as **read**
    
- Old notifications remain visible for reference
