The **Manage Vouchers** section allows admins to create, assign, update, and track all employee vouchers in one place. It is the control center for managing voucher distribution across your organization.

##### **Where to Find It**

From the left menu, click **Vouchers → Manage Vouchers** to open your approval dashboard.

![[nexpense_vouchers_manage_vouchers_v1.png]]

##### **What You Can Do Here**

##### **1. Search & Filter Vouchers**

- **Search bar** → Find vouchers by employee name, email, or voucher details.
    
- **Filter by Employee** → View vouchers assigned to a specific employee.
    
- **Filter by Status** → Choose between:
    
    - **Active** – Voucher has full balance remaining
        
    - **Partial** – Voucher is partly used
        
    - **Exhausted** – Full amount is used
        
    - **Returned** – Voucher returned by employee
        
- **Refresh** → Reloads data to ensure latest updates.
    

##### **2. View Voucher List**

Each row shows complete voucher details:

- **Voucher Code**
    
- **Employee Name**
    
- **Total Amount Issued**
    
- **Used Amount**
    
- **Remaining Balance**
    
- **Status Tag**
    
    - _Active_
        
    - _Partial_
        
    - _Exhausted_
        
- **Created Date & Time**
    
- **Actions (Eye Icon)** – View complete voucher details.

##### **3. Create a New Voucher**

Use this popup to issue a new voucher to an employee.

- **Employee** – Select the employee who will receive the voucher.
    
- **Total Amount** – Enter the value of the voucher.
    
- **Currency** – Choose the currency (default: INR).
    
- **Voucher Code (Optional)** – Add a custom code if needed.
    

Click **Create** to issue the voucher or **Cancel** to close the popup.

![[nexpense_vouchers_manage_vouchers_create_v1.png]]

##### **Why This Page is Useful**

- Helps admins keep track of voucher spending.
    
- Makes it easy to monitor who is utilizing benefits.
    
- Ensures transparency and control over issued vouchers.
