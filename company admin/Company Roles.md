## Open Company Roles


### Step 1: Open Side Menu (If Closed)

- If the menu is hidden, tap the **menu / sidebar icon** to open it.
### Step 2: Locate Company Roles

- In the left navigation panel, scroll if needed.
    
- Find the menu option **Company Roles**.

![[nexpense_company_admin_expenses_company_roles_v1.png|250]]

### Step 3: Click Company Roles
### Step 4: View Company Roles Page
You can now:

- View existing company roles
    
- Create new roles
    
- Edit or manage role permissions

![[nexpense_company_admin_expenses_company_roles_2_v1.png]]

## How to Create a New Role

### Step 1: Open Company Roles

### Step 2: Click Create Role

- On the top-right corner, click **+ Create Role**.

![[nexpense_company_admin_expenses_company_roles_C_1_v1.png]]

### Step 3: Create New Role Window Opens

- A popup titled **Create New Role** appears.

![[nexpense_company_admin_expenses_company_roles_C_2_v1.png|400]]

### Step 4: Enter Role Name

- In **Role Name***, enter the role name  
    (example: _Finance Controller_, _Manager_, _Team Lead_).
    
- This field is **mandatory**.
-
### Step 5: Enter Description (Optional)

- In **Description**, describe the authority or responsibility of the role.

### Step 6: Save the Role

- Click **Save Role** to create the role.
