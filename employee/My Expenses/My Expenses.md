##### **My Expenses**

The **My Expenses** section allows employees to view, manage, and organize all their expense entries in one place.

You can access **My Expenses** from the **left-side navigation menu** of the Employee Portal.

- Open the side menu (if collapsed).
    
- Click on **My Expenses** â€” represented by a **document icon**.
    
- This will take you to your complete list of expense entries.

##### **What You Can Do Here**
- View all your expenses in card format (Draft, Submitted, Approved).
    
- Each card shows **vendor**, **category**, **amount**, **date**, and **linked report**.
    
- Click a card to **open, edit, or review** the expense.
    
- Use **Filters** (top-right) to quickly sort by date, category, or status.
    
- Click **Add Expense** (top-right or bottom-right green button) to create a new expense.
    
![[nexpense_my_expense_overview_v1 .png]]

### **Add Expense**

- You can add a new expense using **either**:
    
    - The **Add Expense** button at the top-right, or
        
    - The **floating + button** at the bottom-right.
        
- Clicking either option opens the **Create Expense** form where you can enter details like date, amount, category, vendor, and receipts.

### **Expense Report Summary (Short Version)**

This page gives a quick overview of all expenses included in your report.

##### **Header**

- Shows report name, period, status, and **total amount**.
    
- Click **Submit Report** to send for approval.
    

##### **Expense Breakdown**

- Pie chart and bar chart show category-wise spending.
    
- Helps you understand where the most money is spent.
    

##### **Expenses List**

- Shows each expense with its category, vendor, date, amount, and receipt status.
    
- You can **edit or delete** any entry from here.
    

##### **Add Expense**

Use the **Add Expense** button to add new expenses directly into the report.
