## **Expense Breakdown 

The **Expense Breakdown** section shows a **visual summary of your expenses**.  
It helps you understand **where your money is spent**.

## **How to Use This Section**

1. Open the **Dashboard**
    
2. Go to **Expense Breakdown**
    
3. Select a view from the dropdown:
     
    - Category / Report / Cost Centre / Project

![[nexpense_employee_dashboard_filter.png]]

4. Analyze your spending visually

## **Recent Reports** 

The **Recent Reports** section shows a list of your **latest expense reports**.  
It helps you quickly check the **status and amount** of each report.

- Scroll to see **Recent Reports**
    
- Click any report to:
    
    - View details
        
    - Edit (if Draft)
        
    - Track approval status
