## Open Reports

- Login to **NexPense Employee Portal**.
    
- From the left menu, click **Reports**.

![[nexpense_ employee_Report_overview_1_v1.png|250]]


### Step 2: View My Reports Page

- The **My Reports** page opens.
    
- All your created expense reports are listed here

![[nexpense_ employee_Report_overview_2_v1.png]]

##  Create a New Report [[Create Report]]

- Click **Create Report** (top-right).
    
- The **Create New Report** page opens.

## Use Filters (Optional)

- Click **Filters**.
    
- Select required filter options (date or status).
    
- Reports list updates automatically.

![[nexpense_ employee_Report_Filter_overview_1_v1.png]]


### View a Report

- Click the **View (eye) icon** next to a report.
    
- The **Report Details** page opens.


### step 1: Select Report Status

- Click the **Status** dropdown.
    
- Choose one option: **Draft / Submitted / Pending / Approved / Rejected**.
    
- The report list updates based on the selected status.
### Step 2: Select Date Range (Quick Options)

- Choose a date range:
    
    - **All Time**
        
    - **This Month**
        
    - **Last Month**
        
    - **3 Months**
        
- Reports update automatically.
### Step 3: Select Custom Date Range

- Click **Custom**.
    
- Choose **Start Date** and **End Date** using the calendar.
    
- Reports are filtered for the selected period.
### Step 4: View Filtered Reports

- Scroll down.
    
- View the reports matching the selected filters.
### Step 6: Clear Filters

- Click **Clear All** to remove all applied filters.
    
- The full report list is shown again.

## **Delete a Report**:

Here are the **simple step-by-step user guide steps to delete a report**:

1. Go to **My Reports** page.
    
2. Find the report you want to delete from the list.
    
3. Click the **Delete (üóëÔ∏è) icon** on the right side of that report.

![[nexpense_ employee_Report_D_overview_2_v1.png]]

4.  A confirmation popup will appear asking to confirm deletion.

![[nexpense_ employee_Report_D_overview_1_v1.png]]


Click **OK** to permanently delete the report, or **Cancel** to stop.
