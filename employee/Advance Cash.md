## Open Advance Cash Page

1. Login to **Nexpense Employee Portal**
    
2. Look at the **left side menu**
    
3. Click **Advance Cash**
    
![[nexpense_ employee_Advance_overview_1_v1.png|300]]

The Advance Cash page will open

### Understand the Page Sections

This page has **two parts**:

![[nexpense_ employee_Advance_overview_2_v1.png]]

### üîπ Top Section ‚Äì Create Advance

Used to **request new advance money**

### üîπ Bottom Section ‚Äì My Advances

### Create Advance

### Step 1: Enter Amount

- Click on **Amount**
    
- Type the amount you need  
    Example: `10000`

### Step 2: Select Currency

- Click **Currency**
    
- Select **INR** (default)

![[nexpense_ employee_Advance_F_overview_1_v1.png]]

### Step 3: Select Project (Optional)

- Click **Project**
    
- Choose a project **if required**
    
- If not required, keep **None**

### Step 4: Select Cost Centre (Optional)

- Click **Cost Centre**
    
- Select a cost centre (example: IT Support)
    
- Or keep **None**

![[nexpense_ employee_Advance_F_overview_2_v1.png]]

### Step 5: Click Create

- After filling all details
    
- Click the **Create** button (green)
    

‚úÖ Your advance request is submitted

- Your advance is approved or active
    
- You can now use this amount for expenses
### What to Do After Getting Advance

1. Spend money for **official work**
    
2. Go to **Create Report**
    
3. Add expenses
    
4. Adjust expenses against the advance
    
5. Submit the report
