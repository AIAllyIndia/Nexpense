The **Pending Approvals** section shows all expense reports that require your review and action.
Click **“View Details”** to open the full list of pending reports and take necessary actions.
    
![[nexpense_employee_pending_approvals_pending_approvals_card_v1.png]]

This page displays all expense reports that are waiting for your approval. You can review, filter, and take action on each report.

---
##### **1. Summary Cards**

- **Pending Items** – Shows the total number of reports awaiting your action.
    
- **Total Value** – Combined monetary value of all pending reports.
    

##### **2. Search & Filters**

Use the filters to quickly find specific reports:

- **Search Bar** – Search by report name or employee name.
    
- **Roles Filter** – Filter based on the role under which you are approving (useful if you have multiple approval roles).
    
- **Level Filter** – Filter by approval level (Level 1, Level 2, etc.).
    
- **Amount Filters** – Enter minimum or maximum amount to filter reports by value.
    
- **Date Filter** – Filter reports within a specific date range.
    
- **Clear Button** – Reset all filters instantly.
    
##### **3. Report Cards**

Each expense report is displayed in an easy-to-read card containing:

- **Submitted By** – Employee who created the report.
    
- **Total Amount** – Total value of the report.
    
- **Expenses Count** – Number of expenses included.
    
- **Date Range** – Period covered by the expenses.
    
- **Approval Role & Level** – Shows the role and level at which you are approving.
    
- **Expand/Collapse Arrow** – Click to view full report details and take actions.
    

##### **4. What You Can Do**

After opening a report, you can:

- **Approve** the expense report
    
- **Reject** it (with a reason)
    
- **Request Changes** from the employee

### **Reviewing a Pending Expense Report**

When you expand a report from the Pending Approvals list, you can view complete details of the submission and take necessary approval actions.

---

#### **1. Report Header**

Displays key report information:

- **Report Title** – Example: _Expense Report – January – Shruti_
    
- **Pending As** – The role under which you are approving (e.g., CE)
    
- **Approval Level** – Level 1, Level 2, etc.
    
- **Submitted By** – Employee name
    
- **Total Amount** – Total value of the report
    
- **Expenses Count** – Number of attached expenses
    
- **Date Range** – Expense period covered
    

---

#### **2. Expense Details**

Each expense within the report is listed with:

- **Vendor / Expense Name** – Example: _Olive JP Nagar_
    
- **Category** – Travel, Food, Office, etc.
    
- **Amount** – Value of that individual expense
    
- **View Button** – Opens the full expense details, including receipt, OCR extraction, and metadata.
    

---

#### **3. Actions You Can Take**

At the bottom of the report, you will see three action buttons:

#### **✓ Approve**

Use this when the report is valid and ready to be approved.

##### **✎ Request Changes**

Use this when you need corrections. Example reasons:

- Missing or unclear receipt
    
- Incorrect amount or category
    
- Additional explanation required
    

The employee will receive your request and can resubmit after making updates.

##### **✕ Reject**

Use this when the report does not comply with company policy or contains invalid expenses (requires a rejection reason).

---

#### **4. Best Practice**

Always **open and review each expense** using the **View** button before taking action. This ensures receipts and details are validated before approval.
