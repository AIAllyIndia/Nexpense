The **Dashboard** is the home screen of the Nexpense app by Ai Ally.  
It provides a quick summary of your expense activity, pending actions, and navigation shortcuts.

</div>



## **1. Greeting & App Header**

At the top of the screen, you’ll see:

- **App Name & Logo** — “Nexpense by Ai Ally”
    
- **Greeting Message** — Personalized, e.g., _“Hi, Tushar”_
    
- A brief description: _“Here’s your expense overview”_
    
- **Notification** — For alerts or updates
    
- [[Settings]] — For managing profile and app settings
    

This section gives you a personalized welcome and easy access to notifications and settings.

---

## **2. Quick Summary Cards**

Below the greeting, the dashboard shows important expense stats:

### **• Rejected Reports**

- Displays the count of rejected expense reports
    
- Helps you quickly identify if any submissions need correction or resubmission
    

### **• Total Approved Amount**

- Shows the total amount approved for reimbursement
    
- Makes it easy to track your cleared claims
    
### **• Drafts in Progress**

- Shows the number of reports that are currently saved as drafts
    
- Helps users know if they have incomplete or pending submissions
    
- Icon: Green briefcase / folder symbol
    

### **•  Reports Submitted**

- Displays how many reports the user has submitted
    
- Useful for tracking monthly or project-wise submissions
    
- Icon: Yellow upload/document icon
These summary cards give instant visibility into your expense activity.

## **3. Create Expense Report**

This section allows you to submit a new report.

- Includes a small icon + title **“Create Expense Report”**
    
- Description: “Group and submit multiple expenses for a period or project.”
    
- A large primary button: **Create Expense Report**
    

This is the main action button for users who want to submit expenses.

![[nepense_dashboard_overview_create_report_v1.png]]

---

## **4. Expense Breakdown**

This section provides an overview of monthly expenses.

- Title: **“Expense Breakdown”**
    
- Includes a **Report** dropdown or filter option
    
- If no expenses are added, it shows: _“No expenses this month”_
    

This area helps users view and analyze expenses once added.

![[nepense_dashboard_overview_expense_breakdown_v1.png]]

---

## **5. Bottom Navigation Bar**

At the bottom, the app includes a tab navigation bar with 4 options:

- **Home** — Dashboard overview
    
- **Reports** — View submitted reports
    
- **Expenses** — Add or manage individual expenses
    
- **Profile** — Update personal details and preferences
    
- Center Action Button: **Camera/Scan** for scanning receipts instantly
    

This provides quick access to all major modules of the app.
